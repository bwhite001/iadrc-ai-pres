🤖 What is AI? The Simple Version

AI = A very knowledgeable assistant you can type questions to
  • Like having an Elmer available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
  • It has read millions of books, manuals, and radio guides
  • You type a question — it responds within seconds
  • Can answer in plain English — no jargon unless you want it
  • It never gets impatient — ask the same question 10 times if needed
  • The most useful tools are completely FREE

📡 Example 1 — Antenna Design Help

Type this into ChatGPT:

💬 Your question:
"I'm a ham in Brisbane, Australia. Calculate the wire lengths for a half-wave dipole for the 40-metre band. I can hang it between two trees about 8 metres high. What wire and connectors do I need?"

AI will give you:

  • Exact wire lengths in metres and feet
  • Simple materials list with local alternatives
  • Tips for your specific height and layout
  • Feeding options — coax, balun, what to buy
📐
468 ÷ f(MHz)
= total dipole length in feet
468 ÷ 7.1 MHz
= 65.9 feet
(≈ 20.1 metres total)

AI calculates this in seconds — no formula needed!

🌏 Propagation Prediction with WSPR

WSPR = Weak Signal Propagation Reporter
(pronounced "whisper")
  • A tiny automated beacon — even just 200 mW!
  • Stations worldwide hear and report your signal
  • Creates a real-time propagation map
  • Free to view at wspr.rocks
  • AI can interpret what the map means for your plans

🔒 Safety Tips for Using AI Online

✅

Only use trusted sites: chat.openai.com or claude.ai — bookmark them right now

❌

Never enter your bank details, passwords, Medicare number, or home address into any AI tool

⚠️

AI can make honest mistakes — always double-check important technical information before acting on it

🎣

Watch out for fake AI websites — scammers use AI brand names. When in doubt, ask a family member or club member first

📧

Consider using a separate email for AI tools — e.g. a free Gmail created just for this purpose

⚠️ What AI Gets Wrong

Know the limits

🎭 "Hallucinations"

AI sometimes makes things up — confidently! Part numbers, regulations, and specs can be wrong. Always verify.

📅 Outdated Information

Training data has a cutoff date. New band plans, equipment releases, and regulations may not be known to the AI.

🔇 Can't Hear or See

AI cannot listen to your audio, read your S-meter, or measure your SWR. It advises based only on what you describe.

📚

The Golden Rule

For important technical decisions, always verify with:

• Your Elmer
• ARRL Handbook
• WIA website
• Manufacturer specs

Use AI as a starting point — not as the final word
